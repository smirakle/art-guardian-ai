# TSMO AI Art Protection Browser Extension

A powerful browser extension that protects digital artwork from unauthorized AI training with one-click protection functionality.

## Features

- **One-Click Protection**: Right-click any image to protect it with TSMO AI protection
- **Automatic Detection**: Automatically identifies and highlights protectable artwork on web pages
- **Platform Integration**: Enhanced features for popular art platforms (ArtStation, DeviantArt, Behance, etc.)
- **Real-time Notifications**: Get instant feedback on protection actions
- **Usage Analytics**: Track your protection activity and statistics
- **Smart Filtering**: Automatically skips UI elements and focuses on actual artwork

## Installation

### Chrome Web Store (Recommended)
1. Visit the Chrome Web Store
2. Search for "TSMO AI Art Protection"
3. Click "Add to Chrome"
4. Follow the installation prompts

### Manual Installation (Development)
1. Download the extension files
2. Open Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`
3. Enable "Developer mode" in the top right
4. Click "Load unpacked" and select the extension folder
5. The extension will appear in your browser toolbar

### Firefox Add-ons
1. Visit Firefox Add-ons
2. Search for "TSMO AI Art Protection"
3. Click "Add to Firefox"

## Setup

1. **Connect Your Account**: Click the extension icon and sign in to your TSMO account
2. **Configure Settings**: Access the settings page to customize detection and notification preferences
3. **Start Protecting**: Browse artwork and use the protection features

## How to Use

### Basic Protection
1. **Right-click Method**: Right-click any image and select "Protect with TSMO AI Protection"
2. **Overlay Buttons**: Look for green "Protect" buttons that appear over detected artwork
3. **Page Protection**: Click the extension icon and choose "Protect This Page" to scan all images

### Advanced Features
- **Auto-Detection**: Enable in settings to automatically highlight protectable images
- **Platform Features**: Enhanced functionality on supported art platforms
- **Bulk Protection**: Protect multiple images at once on art platforms

### Settings Configuration
Access the settings page by:
1. Right-clicking the extension icon
2. Selecting "Options"
3. Configure your preferences for detection, notifications, and account settings

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Extension not working:**
1. Ensure you're signed in to your TSMO account
2. Check that the extension is enabled in your browser settings
3. Refresh the page and try again

**Images not being detected:**
1. Check minimum image size settings
2. Ensure auto-detection is enabled
3. Some images may be excluded to avoid UI elements

**Protection requests failing:**
1. Verify your internet connection
2. Check your TSMO account subscription status
3. Ensure you haven't exceeded rate limits
