## 3. Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) Model

### 5-Year Financial Projections:
```
Year 1: Revenue $1.58M, EBITDA -$800K (-51%)
Year 2: Revenue $8.75M, EBITDA $1.75M (20%)
Year 3: Revenue $23.5M, EBITDA $7.05M (30%)
Year 4: Revenue $47.0M, EBITDA $16.45M (35%)
Year 5: Revenue $75.2M, EBITDA $30.08M (40%)
```

### DCF Calculation:
- **Discount Rate**: 12% (early-stage SaaS risk profile)
- **Terminal Growth Rate**: 3%
- **Terminal Value**: $30.08M × (1+3%) ÷ (12%-3%) = $344M
- **Present Value of Cash Flows**: $12.8M
- **Terminal Value PV**: $195M
- **Enterprise Value**: $207.8M
- **Probability-Adjusted Value**: $207.8M × 60% = $124.7M

**Risk-Adjusted Valuation Range**: $25M - $125M
**Conservative Pre-Money**: $15M (significant discount for execution risk)
